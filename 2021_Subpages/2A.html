WHS INFO

Workplace Health and Safety is a very important aspect of all jobs, and should not be forgotten simply due to the work being a sit down job.
In the Game Dev Industry, electricity and cables will be the most likely hazards, such as electrocution, electrical fires, and tripping over cables. However, other injuries are far more sinister, and not thought about as much, such as back injuries, due to incorrect posture when sitting down, as well as carpal tunnel syndrome, from typing and using a mouse for extended periods of time.
