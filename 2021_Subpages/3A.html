Design Diorama

The Task was to design a 3d diorama, given some basic prompts, and a few media idea, being a game, diorama, or gif/movie.
I opted to create a dungoen diorama, based off of french prisons and fantasy dungeons used in popular culture. the colors were to be very cold blues, and greys, only broken up by a few light sources.
